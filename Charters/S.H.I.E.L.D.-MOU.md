# Memorandum of Understanding: S.H.I.E.L.D. Protocol

**Subject:** Implementation of Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) Biometric Safety Nets for Vulnerable Populations

## 1. Purpose & Alignment
This MOU establishes the technical framework for providing survival resources (food, hygiene, shelter) while maintaining strict compliance with local immigrant protection ordinances. It provides a "Digital Sanctuary" ensuring residents can access aid without creating a centralized paper trail.

## 2. Technical Protection: The ZKP "Invisible Ledger"
* **Privacy by Design:** The Biometric Card verifies that the holder is a qualified resident in need without storing or transmitting their name, address, or immigration status.
* **Zero PII Leakage:** The decentralized database contains only randomized cryptographic hashes. It is mathematically impossible to "re-identify" a user from the ledger.
* **The Biometric Key:** The card's NFC lock ensures that if the physical card is seized or stolen, the survival funds remain completely inaccessible.

## 3. Civic Vitality Tracking
Instead of tracking individuals, the Relay Pulse tracks **Civic Vitality**—the aggregate relational density and resilience of a neighborhood. This allows local government partners to see the *impact* of the aid without ever seeing the *identities* of the recipients.
