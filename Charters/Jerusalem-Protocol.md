# The Jerusalem Protocol: The R&D Innovation Hub

This charter establishes the **"Jerusalem Node"**—a centralized research and development hub funded by the decentralized network to ensure perpetual system improvement. 

## 1. The Mandate of the "Open Ear" (Passive Sentinel)
The R&D Hub operates strictly as a **Passive Sentinel**. It does not dictate innovation from the top down; it reflects the collective needs of the street level.
* **Aggregate Sentiment Nodes:** The AI monitors the P2P network for technical friction (bottlenecks) and operational successes.
* **Demand-Driven R&D:** The hub is technically prohibited from pushing updates to the Relay Pulse unless the innovation solves a specific problem identified by the local "Listening Nodes" (social workers and users).

## 2. Reinvestment Funding
The Jerusalem Node is funded by the "Over-Flow" of the independent forks to prevent resource hoarding.
* **The 120% Threshold:** When a local fork achieves a surplus exceeding 120% of its operational reserve, the excess is Relayed to the R&D Hub.
* **Restricted Use:** These funds are strictly system-only, dedicated to refining the Guardian AI, improving P2P network stability, and ensuring the Public Ledger remains immutable.

## 3. The Open-Source Promise
Every innovation birthed in the R&D Hub is immediately open-sourced and pushed back to all active Forks. Any R&D performed in secret triggers a mandatory "Davidic Reset," empowering the Steward of the Flow to redistribute those funds back to the community.
