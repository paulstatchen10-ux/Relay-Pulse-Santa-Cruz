# Relay Pulse: Santa Cruz
**A Decentralized, AI-Augmented Mutual Aid Network**

Relay Pulse is a first-of-its-kind "Forking" nonprofit model designed to eliminate bureaucratic waste and provide foolproof, direct aid to the unhoused and vulnerable populations. By integrating **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP)** and **Biometric NFC technology**, we create a survival safety net that is invisible to federal overreach and 100% transparent to local donors.

## 🏛️ Architecture: The 4-Fork Fractal
To prevent organizational bloat, the project is split into independent geographic cells (Forks).
* **The Twelve Pillars:** Each fork is managed by exactly 3 people (Director, CFO, Human Connections). A standard 4-fork county setup totals 12 staff.
* **The Steward of the Flow:** A roving role that monitors the **Relay Pulse** to balance resources between forks, ensuring no surplus hoarding occurs while neighboring districts face deficits.
* **Scaling:** When a fork hits 1,000 active users, it must "fork" into a new cell rather than expanding its administrative footprint.

## 🛡️ Key Technologies
* **Biometric NFC Cards:** High-security cards locked by a thumbprint to prevent theft or bartering.
* **MCC Smart-Filtering:** Funds are mathematically locked to specific Merchant Category Codes (e.g., grocery stores, pharmacies, transit) to guarantee aid is used only for survival essentials, eliminating the need for manual receipt auditing.
* **Privacy Shield (ZKP):** Verifies eligibility without storing personal data, creating a "Digital Sanctuary."
* **Relay Pulse (P2P):** A peer-to-peer heartbeat that shares fraud alerts and resource balances across the network.
* **Guardian AI:** An oversight layer that audits every transaction to protect the **90% Stewardship Standard** (90 cents of every dollar reaches the street).

## 🗺️ The California Scaling Vision: "Project Thousand-Fold"
The Relay Pulse is designed to be fractal. To solve homelessness for the roughly 187,000 unhoused Californians:
* **187 Forks:** One independent cell for every 1,000 people.
* **580 Total Staff:** 561 local pillars and 19 Regional Stewards.
* **The $1 Billion Solution:** Raising $933 Million annually allows us to distribute $840 Million in direct survival aid while strictly capping overhead at 10%.
